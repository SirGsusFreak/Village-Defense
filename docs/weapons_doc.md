**Weapons:**

* **Weapon Switch:** Use the mouse wheel to scroll up or down to switch weapons.

* **Type:** Melee

  ### **2\. Pistol**

* **Type:** Ranged (Single Fire)  
* **Attack Speed:** Moderate

  ### **3\. SMG (Submachine Gun)**

* **Type:** Ranged (Automatic Fire)  
* **Attack Speed:** High
